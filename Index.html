Welcome to the Reaction Time Game!!

You will be assessed 2 ways; visually and audibly

When you see the circle change color from red to green, click the circle as fast as you can

The circle will change color 5 times

Once all 5 visual times have been completed, the audible assessment will begin

When you hear the beep, click the circle as fast as you can

The beep will sound 5 times

Click the button below to start the game
